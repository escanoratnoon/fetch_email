Have you tried signing out of the app and then signing back in? Try the following steps.

 

  • Log out of the device
  • Wait a minute and log back in

  • Tap More
  • Tap Ticket Storage; the ticket should show saved to the cloud
  • Tap the icon to the right of the ticket to save it to the device
